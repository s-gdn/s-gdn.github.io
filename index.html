AI Inbox +

_______________________________________________________________________________

AI Inboxüì© is your personal email assistant, able to compose high quality emails in just a couple seconds! This program uses IMAP protocol, Gmail API and Outlook API to retrieve your inbox from any email provider of your choice, which can then be viewed in the built in browser.

The training section of the program allows you to train the AI to your liking, giving it price lists, contact info, how it should sound, or anything you might want. It uses the selected email as context, as well as your training data to compose a perfect email exactly how you want everytime, in just one click. Of course you can also edit the email yourself, or compose your own. Then, using SMTP protocol, the email is sent to your chosen recipient.

Upon signing up to the website, you will be given some free credits used for email generation. If you purchase monthly premium on the website (Stripe checkout implementation), you will be give more credits each month, as well as access to more training features.
